Easy Brownies

For whenever you need a quick pick-me-up, here's a simple recipe for some brownies that you can top with sprinkles, chocolate or whipped cream.

What You Will Need:

  • A bowl.
  • 9x13 inch Pan.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of white sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of melted butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 walnut halves

Steps:

  1. Gather your ingredients
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F--175 degrees C. Grease a 9x13 in pan.
  3. Mix sugar, flour, melted butter, eggs, cocoa powder, vanilla, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl until combined.
  4. Spread batter into the prepared pan.
  5. Optional: Decorate with walnut halves.
  6. Bake until top is dry and the edges have started to pull away, around 20-30 minutes.
